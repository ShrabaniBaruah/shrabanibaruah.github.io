Vahdam × Emily in Paris

Year 2022
Client Vahdam India
Services Art Direction, Creative Direction, Packaging Design
Market Global

The Emily in Paris × Vahdam drop became a media favourite, winning awards and making headlines.

Build a collectible that earns press without buying it. The packaging itself needed to be the campaign.

A suitcase-shaped tea box as the signature piece — iconic and immediately shareable; Parisian glamour meeting Indian craft.

Limited-edition suitcase boxes, inner packaging, campaign material — designed to travel organically across media.

Kyoorius Blue Elephant Award — Packaging Design, 2022. Featured widely across media. A drop that people still talk about.
