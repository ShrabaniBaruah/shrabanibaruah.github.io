Vahdam × Nicole Scherzinger

Year 2022
Client Vahdam India
Services Art Direction, Creative Direction, Packaging Design
Market Global

For Vahdam, I led special-edition packaging for high-impact collabs. With Nicole Scherzinger, the design reflected her love for marble and gold, blending personal style with brand elegance.

Worthy of Nicole's brand and Vahdam's heritage — a genuine collaboration, not a licensing deal. Every design decision needed to feel like her.

Marble and gold; luxurious, tactile, unmistakably personal. Designed to be kept long after the tea is gone.

Limited-edition boxes, inner packaging, campaign photography, social content — a collection with collectible presence.

Sellout collaboration that introduced Vahdam to Nicole's global fanbase.
