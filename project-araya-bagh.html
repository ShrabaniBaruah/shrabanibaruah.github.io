Araya Bagh

Year 2024
Client Araya Bagh
Services Brand Identity, Creative Direction, Art Direction, Visual Strategy
Market India

Araya Bagh — an abode for celebration in New Delhi. Set against the grandeur of Mughal architecture and the warmth of Indian ceremony, Araya Bagh required a brand that could hold both history and modernity in equal measure.

The brief called for a visual identity rooted in the richness of Indian heritage — jali screens, ornamental umbrellas, hand-crafted detail — yet refined enough for a contemporary luxury audience. The result is a brand world that honours tradition without being bound by it.

Modern India Design.

Bridge Mughal heritage with the emotional weight of Indian celebration — timeless without being frozen. The brand needed to hold history and modernity with equal confidence.

Jali-screen geometry in ivory and antique gold; a visual language that signals heritage without nostalgia, ornate without ostentation.

Leather-bound menus, embossed stationery, silk letterheads, copper collateral, digital — every material chosen to match the grandeur of the venue.

A brand that earned its place alongside the architecture and ambition of Araya Bagh itself.
