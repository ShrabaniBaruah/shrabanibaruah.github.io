Science of Snacks

Year 2024
Client Science of Snacks
Services Brand Identity, Creative Direction, Art Direction, Illustration Direction
Market India

Science of Snacks (SOS) is a healthy snack brand built on a simple idea: great snacks are made with real science. The brief called for a brand that could make nutrition feel fun — something that wore its ingredient story on its sleeve without taking itself too seriously.

The identity leans into the science-lab concept with a multi-colour formula system, a cast of illustrated ingredient characters, and a bubbly wordmark that signals delight before you've even read it. Every flavour gets its own colour, every snack its own formula.

Make science the story. Ingredient transparency is the competitive advantage; the personality should be impossible to ignore on shelf.
