Vahdam India

Year 2022
Client Vahdam India
Services Brand Identity,
Creative Direction,
Art Direction,
Packaging Design
Market Global

Vahdam India is one of the world's most recognised premium tea brands, delivering single-origin teas direct from source to over 100 countries. The challenge was to build a visual identity that matched the brand's ambition — a language as refined and confident as the teas themselves.

The identity centres on a deep forest green and warm gold palette, grounding the brand in the landscape of Indian tea estates while projecting a global luxury sensibility. Every touchpoint — from packaging to digital — carries the same quiet authority.
