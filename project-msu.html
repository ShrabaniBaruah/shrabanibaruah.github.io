Overview

This identity was inspired by rolling curves, flowing fabrics and the pleating of a saree — a visual language built to show the interconnectedness of people and sarees, and the rhythmic motion of a saree unfurling, similar to the ebb and flow of vast waves.

The beauty of vernacular typography — Kannada script — was woven into the letterforms to bring in a sense of origin story: present in an extremely subtle, almost hidden form.
