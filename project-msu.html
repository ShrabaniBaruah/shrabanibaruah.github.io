MSU

Year 2024
Client Mysore Saree Udyog
Services Brand Identity, Creative Direction, Art Direction
Market India

This identity was inspired by rolling curves, flowing fabrics and the pleating of a saree — a visual language built to show the interconnectedness of people and sarees, and the rhythmic motion of a saree unfurling, similar to the ebb and flow of vast waves.

The beauty of vernacular typography — Kannada script — was woven into the letterforms to bring in a sense of origin story: present in an extremely subtle, almost hidden form.

Honour 90 years of craft without feeling old. Speak to a contemporary luxury audience that values authenticity as much as aesthetics.

Wordmark drawn from the flow of Kannada script and the pleating of a saree; rhythmic curves and a flexible badge system that works at every scale.

Hang tags, packaging, The Drapery Edit and Nine Yards of Joy campaign photography, brand book, in-store graphics.

A heritage brand made relevant to the modern Indian luxury consumer — proud of its past, confident about its future.
