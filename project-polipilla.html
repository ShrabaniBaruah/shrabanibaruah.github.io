Polipilla

Year 2023
Client Polipilla
Services Brand Identity, Creative Direction, Art Direction, Visual Strategy
Market International

Taking inspiration from the name itself, Polipilla is a dance between the magnanimous octopus and the petite butterfly. They hail from the two different worlds of the Sky and the Sea. Two magnificent, untamed entities with all the freedom in the world. And they co-exist beautifully. This concept follows the beautiful relationship these two worlds have created.

Playing in the space of dual nature of this ecosystem, Polipilla portrays a visual of the two worlds — the Sky and the Ocean — and the other elements co-existing beautifully.

Balancing the effortless with stylish, confident spirit with comfort. This is Polipilla.

Position at the intersection of freedom and duality — for those who move between worlds and refuse a single identity. The brand should feel as effortless as it is bold.

Fluid ink washes meet delicate linework; deep ocean blues set against sky. The wordmark is confident yet airy, shifting comfortably between bold and ethereal.

Apparel labels, packaging, lookbooks, campaign imagery — each touchpoint as multidimensional as the brand world itself.

A cohesive visual world that resonates with a global fashion audience drawn to brands that resist easy categorisation.
