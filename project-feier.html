Feier

Year 2024
Client Feier
Services Brand Identity, Creative Direction, Art Direction, Campaign Direction
Market Global

Feier is a seamless activewear brand built for people who move through life without switching gears — from work to workout to everywhere in between. The identity needed to feel as versatile and high-energy as the clothing itself.

The system centres on a bold electric palette, a stacked stepped mark, and typography that shouts with purpose. Every touchpoint — from hang tags to campaign posters — carries the same restless, joyful energy.

Own the in-between — not performance wear, not casual wear. Feier is for people who never stop moving and refuse to change gears to do it.

Bold electric palette, stacked stepped mark, purposeful typography that shouts with intent. Works equally on billboard and hang tag.

Apparel labels, hang tags, campaign posters, merchandise, social media, digital — a system built to carry the same restless energy everywhere.

Visual impact strong enough to compete in a crowded category. Noticed, remembered, and worn with pride.
