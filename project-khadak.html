Khadak

Year 2022
Client Khadak
Services Brand Identity, Creative Direction, Graphic Design, Web Design
Market UAE & UK

Khadak — meaning crisp, bold, unapologetic — is an Indian street food restaurant concept launching across UAE and the UK. The brief was to build a brand that felt as alive as the culture it draws from: chaotic, warm, layered, and unmistakably South Asian.

The challenge was to move beyond nostalgia-for-nostalgia's-sake and the sanitised "modern Indian" aesthetic that has become familiar in the diaspora dining scene. Khadak needed to feel like a place — specific, textured, with a point of view.

Don't sanitise — lean into the chaotic warmth of Indian street food culture. The brand should feel lived-in from day one, specific to a place and a point of view.

Vernacular fragments — stamp culture, newspaper print, Bollywood typography — layered with intent. Every element tells part of a larger story without tipping into pastiche.

Menus, packaging, uniforms, environmental graphics, khadak.com — a complete brand world carried consistently into every touchpoint.

Specific, textured, and unmistakably alive. Abby Awards 2023 winner for Craft in Design.

The Work

The visual system draws from the layered vernacular of Indian signage, print, and packaging — chai-stall hand-lettering, Bollywood typography, newspaper wrapping, stamp culture. Each element was treated as a fragment of a larger story, assembled into a brand world that rewards attention.

The collateral system spans menus, packaging, stamps, badges, and environmental graphics — all anchored by a restrained core identity that lets the richness of the references breathe without tipping into pastiche.

Website

The brand identity extends into a full digital presence at khadak.com ↗ — a site that brings the restaurant's world to life through immersive storytelling, character pages, a full menu, and the cultural richness of Bhendi Bazaar, Mumbai.
