Baoji Asian Home

Year 2023
Client Baoji Asian Home
Services Brand Identity, Creative Direction, Art Direction, Illustration Direction
Market India

Baoji — named after the Chinese city of Bǎojī — is an Asian home restaurant built around the warmth of family, food, and culture. The brief called for a brand that felt lived-in and loved: the kind of place where the food tastes like someone's grandmother made it.

The visual identity draws on vintage Chinese illustration and poster culture — the family on the bicycle, the calligraphic script, the ornamental borders — layered with a warmth and wit that makes the brand feel both rooted and contemporary. Make Bao, Not War.

Build from the inside out — warmth first, wit follows. The brand should feel established from day one, like a place people have always known.

Vintage Chinese illustration meets contemporary wit; the family on the bicycle, calligraphic script, ornamental borders — rooted and alive simultaneously.

Exterior signage, menus, branded tableware, packaging, business cards, campaign graphics — a complete world for a neighbourhood institution.

Locals adopted it as their own. A neighbourhood fixture from the moment it opened.
