Baoji Asian Home

Year 2023
Client Baoji Asian Home
Services Brand Identity,
Creative Direction,
Art Direction,
Illustration Direction
Market India

Baoji — named after the Chinese city of Bǎojī — is an Asian home restaurant built around the warmth of family, food, and culture. The brief called for a brand that felt lived-in and loved: the kind of place where the food tastes like someone's grandmother made it.

The visual identity draws on vintage Chinese illustration and poster culture — the family on the bicycle, the calligraphic script, the ornamental borders — layered with a warmth and wit that makes the brand feel both rooted and contemporary. Make Bao, Not War.
